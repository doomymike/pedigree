//:Pedigree Family Generator V0.0

-----Interface Notes/Instructions-----

**Each individual is selectable
**Once selected, an individual can have four options



-----Four options of an Individual-----

**ADD PARENTS**

**ADD PARTNER**

**ADD SIBLING**

**ADD CHILD**

**Once selected, an option requires the use to input
**both required and optional additional information (listed below)



-----Required Additional Information-----

**GENDER (MALE OR FEMALE)**

**LIVING (LIVING OR DEAD)**

**AFFECTED (AFFECTED OR INAFFECTED)**



-----Optional Additional Information-----

**CURRENT FIRSTNAME**

**CURRENT LASTNAME**

**BIRTHDATE**

**LASTNAME AT BIRTH (IF CHANGED DURING MARRIAGE)**
